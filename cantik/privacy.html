# 📜 Cantik – Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2025

Welcome to **Cantik**, your glow-up and self-love AI companion. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Cantik mobile app.

---

## 🔐 What Data We Collect

We only collect the **minimum necessary information** to deliver app features and improve your experience:

| Data Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Anonymous ID** | A random unique identifier (UUID) generated on your device to support anonymous usage. |
| **Thumbnail Uploads** | When you upload a photo, we generate a compressed **thumbnail (.webp)** which is stored in **Supabase Storage** for behavior analytics only. Original files stay **local on your device**. |
| **Google Sign-In** | If you choose to upgrade to Premium, we request Google authentication to create a persistent user ID and connect your account to **RevenueCat** and **Supabase**. |
| **Subscription Data** | Managed by **RevenueCat**, we receive status only (active / expired). We do not store payment or billing information. |

---

## ☁️ Cloud Storage Details

| Service | Purpose | What’s Stored |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Supabase** | Caching thumbnail previews | File path & thumbnail only. No original media. |
| **Supabase Auth (via Google)** | Secure user login | Google ID token & user ID (non-sensitive) |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management | Subscription status, not your billing data |

---

## 🔒 What We Don’t Collect

- ❌ We do **not store full-resolution images**
- ❌ We do **not store passwords**
- ❌ We do **not access contacts, location, or microphone**
- ❌ We do **not track your usage across other apps or websites**

---

## 🔁 Data Sync (Premium Users Only)

If you upgrade, we associate your thumbnail data with your Google ID to allow consistent access across devices and subscription recovery.

---

## 🗑️ How to Delete Your Data

You may:

- Clear app data via your device settings
- Request data deletion by contacting us at **[your@email.com]**
- Delete Supabase-stored thumbnails by logging out

---

## 👩‍⚖️ Your Rights

As a user, you may:

- Request to access or delete your data
- Revoke Google Sign-In authorization at any time
- Unsubscribe via your Play Store account
